Manifesto

The music was always here. The support wasn't.

Filipino bedroom and underground music has always had something worth hearing. What it never had was anyone putting in the work behind it. That's the only reason this label exists.

How We Do It

01

Self-Made

No funding, no outside backing. We built this from zero and figured it out as we went. Still running it the same way.

02

It Stays Yours

We're not here to take a piece of what you made. Your music is yours. We put in the work to get it heard, you keep everything.

03

Still In It

We came up through bedroom recordings and underground shows. We know what this scene looks like from the inside. The music and the people in it are the whole reason this exists.
