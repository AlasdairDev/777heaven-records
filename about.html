Our Story

777heaven Records didn't start in an office. It started in 2024 in a Manila bedroom with a laptop, a few friends, and a shared frustration. We were musicians who lived the highs and lows of self-releasing, and we realized the industry was broken. It treated music like a product and artists like line items. We knew there was a better way.

We moved from late-night sessions to the front lines of the underground scene. To give our artists real power, we partnered with oneRPM. This partnership bridges the gap between total creative freedom and global growth. You get the professional distribution and dashboard tools the majors use, while we stay rooted in the same DIY grit we started with.

This is more than just a label. We are a collective that values authenticity over algorithms and people over metrics. We aren't here to own your masters. We're here to master your ownership. We support the artists pushing boundaries, taking risks, and creating work that actually matters. No suits. No compromise. Just the music.
