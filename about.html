Our Story

Origins

Most of us had been releasing music independently in Manila for years before any of this came together. We knew exactly what it felt like — putting months into a track, dropping it, and watching the algorithm push everything past it like it didn't exist. Filipino underground music has always had something genuinely worth hearing. What it's never had is the infrastructure to back it up properly.

We were already friends, already running in the same local circles, already having the same conversation every time we linked — about how hard it was to cut through, how the real options were either sign something you'd regret later or keep grinding alone with no support behind you. 777heaven was the point where we got tired of that conversation and decided to just build the thing ourselves.

Mission

Every one of us has been on the artist side of this. We know what the Philippine music landscape looks like from that angle — how few real options exist, how easy it is to end up locking yourself into a deal that leaves you with streams you can't account for and music you no longer control. We built 777heaven specifically because we didn't want to recreate any of that.

Every artist we work with keeps their masters, their publishing, and full creative control. We partnered with oneRPM so our artists get real global distribution — Spotify, Apple Music, all of it — reaching listeners internationally, not just locally. We do the actual work of pushing your music. We stay out of your creative process unless you want us in it. That's the deal, and it doesn't change.

Vision

We'll be straight about it — we're still figuring things out as we go. We're a team based in Manila, we're still young, and we're not going to oversell what we are right now. But we know exactly what kind of label we want to be: the one that could've changed things for so many artists in this scene who put in the work and never got the backing they deserved.

The goal is to keep growing on our own terms. More artists on the roster, more reach, eventually more presence beyond Manila. There's so much Filipino underground music that deserves a global audience — that's what we're genuinely working toward, one artist at a time.

01

DIY at Scale

We built this in Manila from zero — no funding, no label backing, just people who cared enough to figure it out. The tools have leveled up since then, but we're still running it the same way: hands-on, direct, and by people who are actually invested in what happens.

02

Your Masters Stay Yours

We've seen how it goes when Filipino artists sign the wrong deals. We're not doing that. Your music is yours — masters, publishing, all of it. We handle the distribution and the push. You keep everything you built.

03

No Hidden Numbers

Payouts shouldn't be a mystery. You get a real dashboard showing exactly where your streams are coming from, what your music is earning, and how each release is performing — no vague reports, no waiting around to find out what you made.
