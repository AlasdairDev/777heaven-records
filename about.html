Our Story

Origins

The name comes directly from The Fool, card zero of the tarot. Not because it sounded good, but because it's exactly how this started. The Fool stands at the edge with everything they own in a small bundle, and steps off. Not reckless. Just fully committed to something most people would call a bad idea. That's the only card that made sense for what we were doing.

Most of us had been putting out music independently for years before any of this came together, spread out across different parts of Luzon. We knew what it felt like: months of work, drop the track, watch the algorithm move past it like nothing was there. Filipino underground music has always had something real in it. The tracks that actually get pushed from here tend to sound identical, built from the same template, engineered to fit a format and nothing else. The underground is the exact opposite of that. What it's never had is anyone putting real weight behind it. We were already friends, already in the same circles, already having the same conversation every time we linked. That the only real options were sign something you'd regret later or keep grinding alone. 777heaven was the moment we got tired of that conversation and stepped off the edge ourselves.

Mission

All of us have been on the artist side of this. We know what the Philippine music landscape looks like from that angle. How few actual options exist, how easy it is to end up locked into a deal that leaves you with streams you can't track and music you no longer own. We built this specifically because we didn't want to recreate any of that.

Every artist we work with keeps their masters, their publishing, and full creative control. We partnered with oneRPM so our releases hit Spotify, Apple Music, and everywhere else, reaching listeners internationally, not just locally. We do the actual work of pushing your music. We stay out of your creative process unless you want us in it. That's the deal, and it doesn't move.

Vision

We'll be honest about where we are: we're still building this as we go. We're young, we're spread across Luzon, and we're not going to oversell what we are right now. But we know exactly the kind of label we want to be. The one this scene always needed and never had.

The roster's grown fast. 39 artists in and still moving. The focus now is reach: getting Filipino underground music past the ceiling it always hits and into the ears of listeners who've never heard Manila sound like this. There's too much here that deserves more than a local audience. We're not stopping until it gets one.

01

Built from Zero

We put this together from different corners of Luzon, from nothing. No funding, no backing from anyone else. Just people who cared enough to figure it out. The tools have leveled up. The approach is the same: hands-on, direct, run by people who are actually invested in what happens to the music.

02

Your Masters Stay Yours

We've watched Filipino artists sign deals they regret. We're not doing that to anyone. Your music is yours: masters, publishing, all of it. We handle the distribution and the push. You keep what you built. That's not a negotiable point.

03

No Hidden Numbers

Payouts shouldn't be a mystery. You get a real dashboard showing where your streams are coming from, what your music is earning, and how each release is tracking. No vague reports. No waiting around to find out what you made.
